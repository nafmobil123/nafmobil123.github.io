Here I am again. The same, small compacted platform. The same horde of half-awake, half-dead creatures in t-shirts, or hoodies or a suit and tie while as its wearer reads a "Daily Times". The same sound of shoes dragging on the ground, the sound of coffee being slurped and the crunch of bacon, egg, and cheese. The man with the black hoodie and ripped jeans hasn't showered again. How he still the confidence to smile at strangers who give him the "stay away from me" look, will always remain a mystery.
